Common Questions

Who qualifies for the Act 60 Young Entrepreneur incentive? ▼

Individuals between the ages of 16 and 35 who are starting a new business in Puerto Rico can apply for significant tax exemptions on their first $500,000 in gross income for a fixed period.

Do I need a local Registered Agent for my PR LLC? ▼

Yes. Puerto Rico law requires every LLC to maintain a Registered Agent with a physical address on the island to receive legal documents and official government notices.

What is the main benefit of an Act 60 Export Services decree? ▼

The most well-known benefit is the 4% fixed corporate income tax rate for eligible businesses that export their services from Puerto Rico to clients outside the island.
