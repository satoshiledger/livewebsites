Act 60 & Residency

How do the 2026 changes affect individual resident investors? ▼

For new applicants after January 1, 2026, a 4% tax applies to PR-sourced dividends, interest, and capital gains. Existing decree holders are grandfathered into current 0% rates.

What is the "Closer Connection" test for 2026? ▼

Beyond spending 183+ days on-island, you must prove your "center of life" is in PR. This includes local banking, voter registration, and shifting primary family/social ties away from the mainland.

Young Entrepreneur Incentive

Who qualifies as a Young Entrepreneur? ▼

Individuals aged 16–35 starting a new business qualify for a 100% tax exemption on the first $500,000 of net income for 3 years, plus property tax waivers.
