Must spend at least 183 days per tax year in Puerto Rico. We help you maintain your closer connection documentation to defend against IRS scrutiny. Mandatory annual contribution of $15,000 to PR nonprofits ($7,500 to child poverty organizations). Proof of payment is required for annual report filing. Contribución anual obligatoria de $15,000 a OSFL de PR ($7,500 a organizaciones de pobreza infantil). Se requiere prueba de pago para el informe anual.

Residency Blackout Period Período de Bloqueo de Residencia
